Lasagna

Description

This is how I make a tasteful and savory lasagna!

Ingredients

  • 10 lasagna noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 5 tablespoons of fresh parsley
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 carton of ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Dice onion and chop ground beef into small chunks
  2. Cook the italian sausage, ground beef, and onion in a skillet on medium heat until ground beef turns brown and crumbly, then add garlic and cook for an additional minute
  3. At the same time as cooking the sausage, beef, and onion, pour noodles into a pot with water, bring to a boil and cook for 5-8 minutes or until noodles are soft
  4. Drain the noodles and beef, add in the tomato paste, tomatoes, water, salt, parsley, and pepper, stir all of the ingredients together, bring to a boil then reduce heat and let simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. In a bowl mix the egg, ricotta cheese, and a pinch of salt
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees, spread the meat sauce into your baking pan, layer 3 noodles, then add the ricotta mixture, sprinkle on the parmesan and mozzarella then repeat the layer two additional times with the remaining meat suace going on top.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes covered, then bake for an additional 30 minutes uncovered, afterwards let it stand for 10 minutes before serving
