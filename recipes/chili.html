Chili

Description

This is a beef and tomato juice chili recipe with two cooking options: the stovetop or in a slow cooker

Ingredients

    2 pounds ground beef 2 cans of tomato juice 1 can pinto beans (preferably drained) 1 cup chopped onion 1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper 1/4 cup chili powder 1 teaspoons ground cumin 2 teaspoons salt 3/4 teaspoon sugar 1/3 teaspoon oregano 1/2 teaspoon pepper 1/6 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper A pinch of mozeralla

Steps

    Start by heating your skillet over medium-high heat, put the 2 pounds of ground beef into the skillet and cook until it turns brown and crumbly, between 5-7 minutes. Transfer browned beef into a pot over medium-high heat and the stir in all of the other ingredients. Aftwewards bring to a boil, once it starts to boil reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for another hour, occasionally stirring.
